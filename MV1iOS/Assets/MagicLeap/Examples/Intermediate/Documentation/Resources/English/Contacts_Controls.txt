<color=#dbfb76><b>Bumper</b></color>
- Reset the contacts panel position.

<color=#dbfb76><b>MLA Bumper Button</b></color>
- Reset the contacts panel position.

<color=#dbfb76><b>MLA Touchpad</b></color>
- Move the cursor around.
- If the cursor is on a button, tap to activate it.
- If the cursor is on a text field, tap to erase the contents.

<color=#dbfb76><b>MLA Keyboard</b></color>
- If the cursor is on a text field, append the entered text unto the text field.

<color=#dbfb76><b>MLA Home Button</b></color>
- Calibrates the controller transform.
