<color=#dbfb76><b>Hometap</b></color>
Toggle the tablet following head pose.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Tablet Touch Ring</b></color>
Rotate through a list of 16 pre-defined colors.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Tablet Touch Ring - Button</b></color>
Clear the canvas.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Drawing</b></color>
Use the pen on the tablet surface to draw with the selected color.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Erasing</b></color>
- Default Pen:  Use the opposite end of the pen while drawing to erase content.
- Both Pens:    Hold the [Back] button on the pen while drawing to erase content.
