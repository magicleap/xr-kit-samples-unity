<color=#dbfb76><b>Bumper</b></color>
Resets the music player UI position in front of the camera.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Trigger</b></color>
- Timeline Slider. The playhead jumps to that location. The content might need to buffer for a short time before playback can continue.

- Volume Slider. Modifies the volume.

- Prev/Next Buttons. Jump to the previous or next track in the playlist.

- Play/Pause Buttons. Plays or pauses the current track. If the media is stopped, the track will start playing from the beginning.

- Suffle Button: Toggles shuffle mode between on and off.

- Repeat Button: Toggles repeat mode between Song, Album and Off.
